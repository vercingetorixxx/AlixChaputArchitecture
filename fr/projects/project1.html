Background and Programme

The first semester of the master's degree is part of the field of study 'Ecology: Pierre et Terre'. The challenge is to design an eco-neighbourhood on the site of the administrative city of the Villette Park. The administrative city is now settled in prefabricated buildings built in the 75s. This unique site is located between the park of the Villette and the dock of the bottom of Rouvray and has a significant potential for the creation of an eco-quartier in Paris.

To be Denected

This project is divided into several stages. Initially, analysis and programming make it possible to propose an urban project that is consistent with the context of the nineteenth arrondissement of Paris. Indeed, cartographic and sociological analysis makes it possible to give shape to the programme.The urban project is taking shape and architectural intentions are emerging. Subsequently, his architectural intentions will take shape with an individual project where the challenge will be to propose a construction based on the use of geo-sourced materials such as stone or earth.
