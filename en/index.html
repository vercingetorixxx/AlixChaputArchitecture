ABOUT ME

My name is Alix Chaput, I am a state-certified architect, and I have been working as a project architect since 2021.


I have developed, through my experience in various firms, a strong interest in the renovation and major rehabilitation of buildings. This mode of practice allows me to combine my passion for understanding built heritage with my commitment to actively contributing to its transformation.


Living in the Paris metropolitan area, I primarily work on projects rooted in this region, where issues of urban densification and the adaptation of existing structures are increasingly prominent. In a context where the challenges of habitability and aligning buildings with environmental requirements are growing, I aim to engage primarily in projects where these concepts resonate concretely and translate into bold proposals. For me, it is about committing, today, to creating urban environments and living spaces that are sustainable, desirable, and inclusive.


As I move forward, I am open to opportunities that will allow me, both professionally and personally, to strengthen my skills and experience in this ambitious direction.
